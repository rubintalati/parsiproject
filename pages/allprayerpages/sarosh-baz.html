Khshnaothra ahunahe mazdāo, Ashem Vohū 1

Pa name yazdan Hormazd Khodae awazuni, gorje khoreh awazayad. Ahura Mazda Khodae, awazun, mehraban, velayatmand, dadar-o dashtar, haft Ameshaspandan, bem rasad. Az hama gunah patet pashemanum, az harche gvnah vahad, kardare kuneshe, mineshne, kuneshne, goft, goft-o shem, goft-o kam, goft-o khar, az an gunah, maneshne gunashne kunashan, tani ravan geti minou, okhe avakhsh pasheman, pa se garzashe pa patet hom. Yatha Ahu Vairyo 5, Ashem Vohu 3.

Fravarāne Mazdayasno Zarathushtrish vidaēvo Ahura-tkaēsho.

now recite the short geh according to the time of day

sunrise to 12:40

hāvan gāh short

Hāvanēe ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha. Sāvanghēe Vīsyāicha ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha.

12:40 to 15:40

rapithwan geh short

Rapithwināi ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha. Frādat-fshawe Zangtumāicha ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha.

15:40 to sunset

uzirin geh short

Uzyeirināi ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha. Frādat-vīrāi dākhyumāicha ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha.

sunset to 00:40

aiwisruthrem geh short

Aiwisrūthremāi aibigayāi ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha. Frādat-vispām-hujyāitēe zarathustrōtemāicha ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha.

00:40 to sunrise

ushahin geh short

Ushahināi ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha. Berejyāi nmānyāicha ashaone ashahe rathwe, yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha.

frasastayaēcha Sraoshane ashyehe, takhmahe, tanu-mānthrahe, darshi-draosh, āhuiryehe, khshnaothra yasnāicha vahmāicha khshnaothrāicha frasastayaēcha, yathā AHū vairyō zaotā frā mē mrūtē, athā ratush ashātchit hacha frā ashava vīdhvāo mraotū.

Sraoshem, Ashem, hurathem, verethrājinem fradat-gaēthem ashavanem ashahe ratūm yazamaide Ahunem vairīm tanūm pāiti, Ahunem vairim tanum pāiti, Ahunem vairim tanūm pāiti, Yathā Ahū Vairyo 1.

Kem nā Mazdā. Yathā Ahū Vairyō 2.


Yasnemcha vahmemcha aojascha zavarecha āfrīnāmi, Sarōshe ashyehe, takhmahe, tanu-mānthra, darshi-draosh, āhūiryehe. Ashem Vohū 1.

May it please the Almighty! In the name of Ahura Mazda, I start my prayer. May 'Sraosha' the holy, mighty leader of all creations, who protects people, come to my help. I repent for any wrong that I may have done and promise not to do anything that is not right. I am a worshipper of Ahura Mazda and a follower of Zarathushtra. I oppose all evil. I worship the strong victorious "Sraosha".
