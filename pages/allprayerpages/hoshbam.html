Ashem Vohū 1

Ahmāi raēscha; Hazanghrem; Jasa me avanghe Mazda; Kerfeh Mozd.

Hoshbam is a homage to day-break. I pray to Ahura Mazda to destroy all evil and help protect the people, cattle, water and vegetation. May the good religion of Zarathushtra spread in every home, village, city and country. May the Ameshaspands and Yazads work for the prosperity of God's creation. May my soul progress and be happy to gain peace through righteousness. May I be worthy of Thy everlasting friendship.
