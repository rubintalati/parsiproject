101 names of ahura mazda

the divine attributes and qualities

The 101 Names of Ahura Mazda (Sad-o-yek Nam-e-Khoda) recounts the many attributes and qualities of the Divine. Each name reveals an aspect of Ahura Mazda's nature, from omniscience and omnipotence to compassion and justice. Reciting these names is a way to contemplate the divine qualities and seek connection with the Creator.
